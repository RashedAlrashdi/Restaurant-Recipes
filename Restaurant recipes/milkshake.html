Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 3 chocolates
  • 250ml milk
  • 3 ice cubes

Method:

  1. add your chocolate and add it to a blender
  2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender
  3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds
  4. Drink!

Blueberry Milkshake
